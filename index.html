About


Welcome to the Bloom in the Theater Festival, where we honor Asian LGBTQ+ people's stories. These stirring tales demonstrate the bravery, tenacity, and delight of negotiating one's identity at the intersection of queerness and culture. We hope that these performances will increase awareness and encourage unity in the struggle for LGBTQ+ rights. We appreciate your support of this important celebration of love and sincerity.
