Bloom In The Theater Festival


About


The goal of Bloom In The Theater Festival is to increase awareness of LGBTQ+ rights, particularly for Asians, as these rights must be upheld to guarantee that no one faces discrimination on the basis of their gender identity or sexual orientation.
